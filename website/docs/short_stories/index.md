---
title: Short Stories
---

Short fiction about technology, identity, and memory.

- [The Mind Clinic](gp.the_mind_clinic.md): An AI engineer checks into a
  wellness clinic for mild burnout and finds himself descending, level by level,
  into something he cannot name

- [The Fog-Colored Suit](gp.a_fog_colored_suit.md): Naples, 1960. A man in a
  pressed suit the color of fog, and what that suit costs him

- [All You Need Is Luck](gp.all_you_need_is_luck.md): A scientist builds a
  machine that reconstructs the past, tracing every hidden branch of fate, and
  discovers what that knowledge costs

- [They Might Be in the Wall](gp.they_might_be_in_the_wall.md): A sound in
  the wall, right before sleep. Deliberate. Alive.

- [A Bug](gp.a_bug.md): A late-night return home, a cockroach crushed underfoot,
  and a quiet reckoning with what it means to share a space
